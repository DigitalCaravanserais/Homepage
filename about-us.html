About us

Makingindigital is a digital hub for showcasing academic projects on digital heritage, using tools such as photogrammetry, 3D modeling and rendering, immersive technologies, as well as gamification/game-based learning. Several teams of designers, architects and programmers work here together with their partners on their fields of application.

What we do ?

Research on preservation and awareness of tangible and intangible heritage by means of field survey. Each project uses many forms and methods as summarized below.

Mapping

Photogrammetry

Augmented Reality

Gamification

Reconstruction

360° Images

Virtual Reality

Public Awareness

Partners and Sponsors

The projects directed by Dr. Güzden Varinlioğlu, in the field of digital heritage, is in partnership with the academicians of the Design Computation Lab, Department of Architecture at Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), Architectural Design Computing at the Istanbul Technical University, Digital Humanities Program at University of California Los Angeles.

Funded by U.S. Department of State, Mission Turkiye Program, Chevening Oxford Centre for Islamic Studies, Abdullah Gül Visiting Scholar Program, Fulbright Visiting Scholar Program, MIT International Science and Technology Initiatives - MISTI Global Seed Funds, TÜBİTAK Visiting Scholar Program, Izmir University of Economics Scientific Research Projects Coordination Unit with the permission of Turkish Ministry of Culture and Tourism, Directorate General of Cultural Assets and Museums.
